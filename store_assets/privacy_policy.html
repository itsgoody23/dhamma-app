Dhamma App — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Dhamma App is a free, open-source application for reading the Pali Canon. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

Without an account (default)

When you use Dhamma App without signing in, we collect no personal data. All your bookmarks, highlights, notes, and reading progress are stored locally on your device only.

With an account (optional)

If you choose to create an account for cross-device sync, we store:

  • Your email address (for authentication)
  • Your bookmarks, highlights, notes, and reading progress (for sync)
  • Your personal translations and annotations (for sync)

Account data is stored on Supabase servers. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the app settings.

Notifications

If you enable daily sutta reminders, notification scheduling is handled locally on your device. No notification tokens or data are sent to external servers.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No advertising identifiers
  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No location data
  • No device fingerprinting
  • No third-party trackers or SDKs

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — Used for optional account authentication and data sync. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign-In / Apple Sign-In — Used for optional social authentication. Subject to Google/Apple privacy policies.
  • YouTube — Dhamma talks are streamed from YouTube. Subject to Google Privacy Policy.

Data Retention

Local data remains on your device until you uninstall the app or clear app data. Account data is retained until you delete your account.

Children's Privacy

Dhamma App does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app contains no age-restricted content.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue at github.com/dhamma-app.
