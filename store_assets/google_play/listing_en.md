## Full Description (4000 chars max)
Dhamma App brings the complete Pali Canon to your pocket — free and offline.

Browse the Tipitaka by Nikāya, search any sutta by keyword or title (diacritics optional), and read with a clean, distraction-free reader. Highlight passages, add bookmarks, write personal notes, and pick up where you left off.

KEY FEATURES

• Complete Pali Canon — Dīgha, Majjhima, Saṃyutta, Aṅguttara, and Khuddaka Nikāyas
• Offline-first — download content packs once, read anywhere without internet
• Powerful search — full-text search with diacritics support (satipaṭṭhāna or satipatthana)
• Daily sutta — a new sutta every day, plus structured reading plans for beginners and advanced students
• Side-by-side Pāli — view translations alongside the original Pāli text
• Personal translations — write your own translations and annotations
• Audio — Pāli chanting, guided meditation (mettā, ānāpānasati), and Dhamma talks
• Pali dictionary — built-in lookup for Pāli words
• Study tools — bookmarks, highlights, notes, and collections
• Sync across devices — sign in to sync your progress (optional)
• Community packages — browse and share translation packages
• Dark mode — comfortable reading day and night
• Multiple languages — English, Deutsch, Français, Español, සිංහල UI

Dhamma App collects no advertising data and has no trackers. Optional sign-in uses Supabase for sync only. See our privacy policy for details.
